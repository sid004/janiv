Janiv Seva: Empowering Communities, Transforming Lives

Together, we can create a brighter future for those in need.

Janiv Seva is a nonprofit organization committed to supporting underprivileged communities through education, healthcare, and social empowerment initiatives. We believe that every individual deserves a chance to live with dignity and hope. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to bring positive change and sustainable development to vulnerable sections of society.

About us

In a world where compassion and care form the foundation of progress, Janiv has been dedicated to uplifting lives across generations. Our commitment extends to four pillars of impact: Well-being and dignity of older individuals, Nourishing deprived, Improving quality of Education, Rural Empowerment, and Healthcare.

Our Vission

A thriving rural education ecosystem is essential for the holistic development of rural communities. By focusing on infrastructure, quality education, community engagement, skill development, health, inclusivity, and policy support, we can create an ecosystem that not only imparts knowledge but also empowers individuals, fosters sustainable development, and contributes to the overall progress of society. At Janiv, we have implemented projects such as water supply, improving infrastructure at schools, creating an employment opportunities for women in the villages of Pachghar, Kasti, Ambhe, Sagroli

Janiv Annachatra

At Janiv Annachatra, we are dedicated to offering nutritious meals at an affordable price of Rs. 15 per meal, ensuring access for those in need. Recognizing the financial challenges faced by the underprivileged, who often struggle to afford daily meals, we have established Annachatra initiatives in 'Diva' on the outskirts of Mumbai and in Nanded. Operating on a non-profit, no-loss basis, these initiatives aim to provide sustenance to the needy within the community. Notably, during the challenging times of the pandemic, we were able to extend our support by providing free meals to approximately 100,000 individuals in need. Currently, Janiv is able to satisfy hunger of around 500 people every day.

Janiv Davakhana

At Janiv Davakhana, we are committed to extending essential medical facilities to those in need. Recognizing the importance of healthcare accessibility, particularly for vulnerable communities, we strive to provide comprehensive medical support. Whether it's through community clinics, outreach programs, or partnerships with healthcare providers, our aim is to ensure that everyone, regardless of their economic status, has access to quality medical care. In times of crisis or adversity, we stand firm in our dedication to make healthcare a fundamental right for all, leaving no one behind. We don’t change a single rupee for the medical services. However, people can donate rice, dal, veggies as per their ability and wish.
